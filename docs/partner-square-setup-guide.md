# Connecting Your Square Account

Bring your Square bookings, services, and more directly onto your partner page — no coding required.

---

## What You'll Need

- An active **Square account** (free to create at squareup.com)
- **Admin access** to your partner page on unicorn.gives
- About **2 minutes**

---

## Step 1: Open Your Partner Settings

1. Sign in to **unicorn.gives** with your admin account
2. From the admin sidebar, click **Partners**
3. Click on your business name (e.g., "The Mane")
4. Click the **Square** tab at the top of the page

You'll see a card that says **"Connect Square"** with a brief description of what connecting enables.

---

## Step 2: Connect Your Square Account

1. Click the **"Connect Square Account"** button
2. You'll be redirected to Square's website to authorize the connection
3. Sign in to your Square account if prompted
4. Review the permissions and click **"Allow"**
5. You'll be redirected back to your partner settings with a success message

That's it — your Square account is now linked!

---

## Step 3: Choose What to Show

Once connected, you'll see your Square integration dashboard with:

- A **green dot** showing your connection status
- **Sync Now** and **Disconnect** buttons
- **Feature toggles** for what appears on your public page

### Available Features

| Feature | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Bookings** | Shows your Square services (with prices and durations) and lets customers book appointments directly from your page |
| **Subscriptions** | Displays membership or subscription plans *(coming soon)* |
| **Retail / Shop** | Shows products from your Square catalog *(coming soon)* |
| **Gift Cards** | Lets customers purchase gift cards *(coming soon)* |

Toggle **Bookings** on to get started. Your services, prices, and team members will be pulled from Square automatically.

---

## Step 4: Sync Your Data

Click **"Sync Now"** to pull in your latest services and team members from Square. This happens automatically when changes are made in Square, but you can trigger it manually anytime.

After syncing, visit your public partner page to see the **"Book an Appointment"** section with your services listed.

---

## How Booking Works for Your Customers

When a customer visits your partner page, they'll see:

1. **Your services** — name, duration, price, and description pulled from Square
2. A **"Book"** button on each service
3. After clicking Book, a step-by-step flow:
   - **Choose a staff member** (or "any available")
   - **Pick a date** from the next 14 days
   - **Select a time** from real-time availability
   - **Enter their info** (name, email, phone)
   - **Confirm** the booking

The booking is created directly in your Square account — it shows up in your Square Dashboard and any appointment management tools you use.

---

## Managing Your Connection

### Sync Now
Pulls the latest services, team members, and availability from Square. Use this after making changes in your Square Dashboard.

### Disconnect
Removes the Square connection and clears all synced data from your partner page. You can reconnect anytime.

### Location
If your Square account has multiple locations, you can choose which one to display on your partner page.

---

## FAQ

**Does this cost anything?**
No. Connecting Square to your partner page is free. Standard Square processing fees still apply to any transactions.

**Can customers pay when booking?**
Payment is collected at the time of the appointment, matching Square Appointments' default behavior. Online payment at booking is coming in a future update.

**What if I change my services in Square?**
Changes sync automatically via webhooks. You can also click "Sync Now" to pull updates immediately.

**Can I disconnect at any time?**
Yes. Click "Disconnect" on the Square tab and the integration is removed immediately. Your Square account is not affected.

**I don't see my services after connecting.**
Make sure you've clicked "Sync Now" and that **Bookings** is toggled on. Your services must be set up in Square with the Appointments service type.
