Local landowners are invited to learn about land protection options such as conservation easements and how to preserve natural features while maintaining property ownership. The program will also feature information on the Forest & Water Fund, which offers financial support for land management and conservation efforts.

Presented by Shelby Gentle, Land Conservation Specialist with Little Forks Conservancy, and Vic Lane, Project Manager with the Michigan Forest Association.

No registration required and no obligation to participate in any conservation program. For more information, contact Shelby Gentle at (989) 835-4886 ext. 103 or sgentle@littleforks.org.
