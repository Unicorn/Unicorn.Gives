This ordinance prohibits recreational marijuana establishments from operating within Lincoln Township. It bans dispensaries, grow operations, processing facilities, and other commercial marijuana businesses. The ordinance was adopted under the authority granted to municipalities by Michigan's recreational marijuana law.
