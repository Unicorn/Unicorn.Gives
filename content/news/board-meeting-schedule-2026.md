The Clare County Board of Commissioners meets on the 3rd Wednesday of each month at 9:00 AM at 225 W Main St, Harrison. 2026 dates: April 15, May 20, June 17, July 15, August 19, September 16, September 30, October 21, November 12, December 16. Meetings are open to the public. Virtual attendance is available via Zoom. Contact (989) 539-2510 for more information.
