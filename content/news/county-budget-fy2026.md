The Clare County Board of Commissioners approved the fiscal year 2026 budget at their December meeting. The budget covers county operations including all departments, courts, and services. County offices are located at 225 W Main St, Harrison, MI 48625. The full budget document is available through the County Administrator's office at (989) 539-2510.
