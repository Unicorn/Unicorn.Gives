The Clare County Drain Commissioner manages county drains, drainage districts, and stormwater infrastructure. Report drainage issues, blocked culverts, or flooding to the Drain Commissioner's office at (989) 539-7320. The office is at 225 W Main St, Harrison. Drainage Board meetings are held as needed to address district-wide matters. For emergency flooding or road washouts, contact Central Dispatch at (989) 539-1336.
