Step-by-step guide to using the Fetch GIS portal at app.fetchgis.com/clare. Search by address or parcel number. View property boundaries, ownership, assessed values, and zoning. The BS&A Online portal (bsaonline.com) provides additional property tax and assessment data. For questions about assessments, contact the Equalization Department.
