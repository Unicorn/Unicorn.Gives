The Clare County Animal Shelter is open for adoptions 10am to 4pm, Monday through Friday. Located in Harrison. Walk-ins welcome to view available animals. Adoption fees vary. The shelter also handles stray animal reports and animal control services. After-hours animal emergencies: (989) 539-1336 (Central Dispatch).
