The Clare County Veterans Services office assists veterans and their families with VA benefits claims, healthcare enrollment, pension applications, and connecting to state and federal resources. Located at 225 W Main St, Harrison. Open Monday through Friday, 8am to 4:30pm. Both Karl Hauser (Director) and Allisha Gary (VSO) can help with claims filing and benefits questions. No appointment needed but recommended for complex claims.
