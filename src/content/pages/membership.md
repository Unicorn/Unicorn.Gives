## Join The Horn

The Horn is developing a membership program for the Lake George community. Membership will offer a way to support The Horn while enjoying perks and having a voice in what happens here.

### Planned Membership Benefits

- **Member-Only Events** — Access to exclusive gatherings and experiences
- **Priority Booking** — First access when reserving The Horn's space for events
- **Discounts on Artisan Goods** — Savings on locally made products available at The Horn
- **Community Voice** — Voting on community programming and future initiatives

### Membership Details

Membership tiers and pricing will be announced soon. To be the first to know when the program launches, subscribe to our mailing list:

**[Subscribe for updates](/subscribe)**

### In the Meantime

Everyone is welcome to visit The Horn and attend public events. You do not need to be a member to be part of what we are building here.
