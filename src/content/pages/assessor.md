## Township Assessor

The Assessor establishes the taxable value of all property in Lincoln Township.

### Contact Information

Contact the township office for assessor services:
- **Phone**: 989-588-9841

### Key Services

- Property assessments
- Property tax information
- Assessment appeals (March Board of Review)
- Homestead exemptions
- Principal residence exemptions

### Important Dates

- **March Board of Review** - Assessment appeals (specific dates to be announced)
- **March** - Assessment notices mailed
