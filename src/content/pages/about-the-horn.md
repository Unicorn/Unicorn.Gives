## The Horn — Community Center & Artisan Goods

The Horn is a community center and artisan goods establishment in the heart of Lake George, Michigan. Housed in the historic former Lake George Grocery building, The Horn serves as a gathering space for events, local makers, and community connection.

### Our Story

The building was purchased in 2021 and underwent extensive renovation to transform it from the old Lake George Grocery into a welcoming community space. The Horn soft launched in 2022 with a focus on bringing people together and supporting local artisans and makers.

The renovation preserved the character of this historic village building while creating a flexible space for events, retail, and gathering.

### What We Offer

- **Artisan Goods** — Locally made and curated products from area makers and small businesses
- **Community Gathering Space** — A welcoming place to connect with neighbors and friends
- **Event Hosting** — Space for community events, workshops, and private bookings
- **A Hub for Lake George** — A place that brings energy and activity back to the village center
