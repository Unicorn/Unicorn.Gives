Planning Commission and Zoning Board of Appeals Meeting Minutes

The Planning Committee Meets the 1st Tuesday (after
the 2nd Mon.) each Month, 7:00 pm.  
ZBA Meetings are scheduled only as needed, and ONLY Approved Minutes may be
posted.  
ZBA Meetings notices are posted at least 15 days before their date.  Check
the homepage for meeting notices.
